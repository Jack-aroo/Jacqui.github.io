About

I am a PhD student at QUT in the Faculty of Health, Clinical Sciences, with my project focusing on using an advanced ultrasound technology called Shear Wave Elastography to evaluate the change in stiffness of tissue. This project will assess the spasticity of muscles in the acute stage post-stroke and positively impact the occupation and physical therapy in their recover. I am also a Senior Sonographer in a private medical imaging company where I am the chairperson of the Quality Assurance and Development Committee, as well as an ASA MSK Special Interest Group member.
